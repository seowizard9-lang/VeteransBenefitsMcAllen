How We Help Connect You with Local Providers

Our goal is to make your journey as simple as possible. While we cannot get a referral *for* you (only your VA provider can do that), we can help you understand the process.

We provide information on this site to empower you when you speak to your doctor and the Community Care office. By knowing the process and having the names of trusted providers handy, you can take control of your healthcare and get your referral approved faster.
