How to Access Your Massage Benefits

This 3-step process can help you get a referral for massage therapy.

1. Talk to Your Physician

Speak with your primary physician at the VA about your conditions (e.g., back pain, stress). Ask if massage therapy is a suitable treatment for you.

2. Get a Referral

If approved, your physician will place a referral. The VA Community Care department will then contact you to coordinate your treatment.

3. Choose Your Provider

Community Care will ask where you want to go. Suggest one of the trusted local providers listed on this site to begin your therapy.
